### Bug Fixes

- Can not play any streams if logged in (YouTube)
- Frequently "Something went wrong" in trending page (YouTube)
- Comments only 1 page (BiliBili)

YouTube has recently implemented complex encryption in its logged-in state extractor, causing PipePipe to malfunction under logged-in conditions. Fortunately, we've discovered a workaround that keeps the login feature operational for the time being. We're collaborating with other open-source YouTube tool communities to develop a more stable decryption solution.
